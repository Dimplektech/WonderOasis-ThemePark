Important Information

General Instructions

  • Always follow the lifeguard's instructions.
  • Wear appropriate swimwear at all times.
  • Do not run near the pool areas to avoid accidents.
  • Keep your belongings in the designated lockers.

Height Restrictions

  • Water slides: Minimum height of 1.2 meters (4 feet).
  • Wave pool: Children under 1 meter must be accompanied by an adult.
  • Lazy river: Suitable for all heights, but children must wear life jackets.

Hygiene Guidelines

  • Shower before entering the pool areas.
  • Do not enter the water if you have open wounds or infections.
  • Dispose of trash in the designated bins to keep the park clean.
  • Ensure children wear swim diapers if necessary.

Lockers and Changing Facilities

  • Secure lockers are available for storing your belongings. Please bring your own lock or rent one at the park.
  • Spacious changing rooms are available for your convenience.
  • Family-friendly changing areas are provided for parents with young children.
  • All facilities are cleaned and sanitized regularly to ensure hygiene and safety.

Water Quality and Hygiene Practices

Water Quality Standards

  • Our water is tested daily to ensure it meets the highest safety and cleanliness standards.
  • We use advanced filtration systems to maintain crystal-clear water in all pools and attractions.
  • Chlorine and pH levels are monitored regularly to ensure a safe swimming environment.
  • Certified professionals oversee water quality management at all times.

Hygiene Practices

  • All visitors are required to shower before entering the pool areas.
  • Hand sanitizing stations are available throughout the park.
  • Regular cleaning and disinfection of high-touch surfaces are conducted throughout the day.
  • Staff members are trained in hygiene protocols to ensure a safe and clean environment for everyone.
