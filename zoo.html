About Our Birds

At WonderOasis Zoo, we house a variety of exotic and colorful birds that captivate visitors of all ages. Learn about their unique behaviors, habitats, and fascinating characteristics.

  • Peacock: Famous for their dazzling tail feathers and courtship displays.
  • Parrot: Highly intelligent birds that can mimic human speech and sounds.
  • Flamingo: Known for their pink feathers, which come from their diet of shrimp and algae.
  • Eagle: Majestic birds of prey with incredible vision and hunting skills.
  • Owl: Nocturnal birds known for their silent flight and sharp hearing.
